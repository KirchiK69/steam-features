Half-Life 2

Gordon Freeman awakens in City 17 and leads a desperate resistance against the Combine. The definitive first-person shooter with unparalleled physics and immersive combat.

About This Game

Half-Life 2 is a landmark first-person shooter that set the standard for narrative-driven action. Featuring groundbreaking physics, intelligent enemies, and a gripping story, it remains a masterpiece over two decades later.

The game includes the episodic expansions Episode One and Episode Two, continuing the story beyond the main campaign.
