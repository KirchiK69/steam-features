# Archives — Digital Preservation

## Problem

Removed games disappear completely, including reviews and historical context.

## Current State

Users lose access to information about previously available products.

## Proposed Solution

- Archive game pages:
  - access without the option to purchase and with separation of reviews before and after the project's closure
- Add status indicators:
  - removed from store  
  - servers shut down  

## Expected Impact

- Preservation of platform history  
- Increased transparency  
- Improved user trust
