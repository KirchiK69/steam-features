# Merch — Community-Driven Merchandise

## Problem

Merchandise is limited or unavailable, reducing community engagement.

## Proposed Solution

- Introduce a built-in merch store in Steam
- Focus on:
  - community-driven products  
  - casual / non-corporate design  
  - strong connection to game franchises  

## Expected Impact

- Increased user engagement  
- Stronger Valve brand  
- Additional interaction channel with the community
