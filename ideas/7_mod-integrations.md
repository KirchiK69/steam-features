# Mod Integrations — Unified Mod Ecosystem

## Problem

The modding ecosystem is fragmented and often relies on third-party tools.

## Current State

Mods and mod managers are distributed via external platforms. Steam Workshop is a step forward but does not cover the full workflow.

## Proposed Solution

- Distribute mod managers as Steam applications (e.g., Nexus Mod Manager)
- Integrate mod managers with Steam Workshop
- Provide centralized installation, updates, and management

## Expected Impact

- Simplified modding experience  
- Increased user engagement  
- Higher quality mod ecosystem
